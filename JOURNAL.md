# 3/19/2026 6 AM - Motor, Battery and, ESC selection

_Time spent: 5h_

I firsh stared with a 2S 5200mAh 50C lipo battery as it is what i have used on previous aerial vehicles and I understand its performace curve and voltage drop quite well. However as i began running the numbers, i realized that loaded with my standard 5"x3" bivane propellers that it would max out at about 6K RPMs which would not produce nearly enough thrust for stable flight and could cause problems in even slight wind. For these calculations, i am assuming 2300kv brushless motros becuase of their relavtivly high efficency compared to weight. I decided to use 20A ESCs due to their lower cost and to avoid overstressing the battery. If i switch to a 3S 2200mAh 50C lipo, I loose some flight time, ~20mins total, however, i produce more thrust and end up being more stable with ~16k RPMs Something else i may try once i have the drone bult is using 3d printed toroidal propellers as they are quieter and lighter.
